HRMIS Hotel Manager

Hotel Management module for HRMIS focused on reservations: staff pick dates, room types, and confirm bookings with live availability and secure flows across web and front desk.

Reservations often rely on scattered tools and manual updates, causing double bookings and stale availability. Staff need a single mobile flow to pick dates, room types, and confirm in real time, with secure handling of guest data and automated confirmations.

  • Reservation management with check-in/out date pickers and room-type selection, supporting web and front desk bookings.
  • Real-time availability synced to the room inventory so managers always see up-to-date options.
  • Automated confirmation emails with booking references and essential stay details.
  • Intuitive mobile UI to navigate dates and room options quickly.
  • Secure flows with HTTPS and role-based access for staff handling guest data.
