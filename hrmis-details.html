HRMIS Customer Support Portal

Customer support module of a Hotel & Restaurant Management Information System. Provides unified customer interactions (bookings, restaurant coordination, chat guidance) so staff and guests stay in sync across rooms, dining, and support.

Hotels and restaurants often run separate systems for bookings, dining, staff, and support. This causes gaps (e.g., a dinner request missed because the restaurant is fully booked) and a poor guest experience. The HRMIS customer support module unifies these touchpoints with real-time visibility across departments.

Built on microservices with a React.js web front-end and Spring Boot APIs, backed by MySQL and containerized with Docker. The module centralizes guest queries, bookings, and restaurant coordination, with chat support for menu guidance and quick answers. Real-time data keeps reception, kitchen, and service teams aligned.

  • Unified web support portal for bookings, dining, and guest queries
  • Chat assistance for menu guidance and quick responses
  • Real-time coordination between reception and restaurant teams
  • Microservices APIs (Spring Boot) with MySQL persistence
  • Containerized with Docker for scalable deployment
