Multimodal Deep Learning for Non-Invasive Diabetes Screening

Hybrid CNN-Transformer model with cross-modal attention that fuses chest X-ray and EHR data for non-invasive Type 2 Diabetes screening, trained with class-balanced strategies to mitigate label imbalance.

Type 2 Diabetes screening often depends on invasive tests and single-modality signals, missing non-linear patterns across imaging and clinical records. Class imbalance further skews model performance and fairness.

Build a hybrid CNN encoder for chest X-rays and a Transformer encoder for EHR sequences, fused with cross-modal attention. Apply class-balanced loss and data sampling to address skewed labels.

Train and tune on ModalAI and Azure ML GPU infrastructure, tracking class-balanced metrics and calibration. Interpretability via Grad-CAM (imaging) and SHAP (EHR) to validate signal contributions.
