HRMIS Virtual Waiter

Virtual Waiter module of the Hotel & Restaurant Management System. Guests browse menus, place orders, confirm, track status, and view bills from their phones with real-time sync to restaurant operations.

Guests browse all dishes and beverages with category filters and rich item details (descriptions, prices, images). The menu stays current via real-time sync, keeping availability and pricing accurate.

Customers add items with quantities and preferences, manage a live cart, and see availability and pricing update instantly for a smooth checkout flow.

Orders are sent to the kitchen via the restaurant order manager with immediate confirmation and real-time status updates for guests.

Line-item pricing and totals update dynamically as guests adjust their cart, so they can review charges before payment with confidence.

Guests follow order progress with real-time updates and notifications for key milestones (prep, ready, served) driven by restaurant staff workflows.
